Engliplay Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 28, 2026

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy outlines how the Engliplay application handles data and information when you use the app and its services.

1. Information Collection and Use

Engliplay is designed to function with the minimum amount of personal data possible:

  • No Account Registration: You can use the application without needing to enter an email address or name.
  • Technical Data: Non-identifying information such as device model and Android version may be collected to ensure proper compatibility with the audio player (ExoPlayer) and web content display (WebView).

2. Local Storage and User Preferences

The app uses the device's local storage (SharedPreferences) to enhance your experience:

  • Language Preferences: Saves your chosen language and interface mode.
  • Personal Dictionary: Words you "star" are saved locally on your device for later review.
  • Progress Tracking: Saves lessons or stories you have reached.

3. Third-Party Services

The application relies on several external services to provide advanced features:

  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements that support the app's continuity. Google may collect your device's advertising ID to provide relevant ads.
  • Firebase: Used as a database to fetch story content, links, and text updates in real-time.
  • Translation API (MyMemory): When you request a translation for a specific word, only that word is sent to the service. This word is not linked to any of your personal data.

4. File Management and Content Protection

Since the app provides a download feature for offline listening:

  • Permissions: The app requests access to its own storage space to save audio files (MP3) and text files (HTML, SRT).
  • Encryption and Security: All downloaded files are stored encrypted to ensure content integrity. The app decrypts them instantly in temporary memory (RAM) only during playback; these files do not appear in their original form in the device's file manager.

5. External Links

The application may contain links to external websites or videos. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of those external sites.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to keep pace with new features in the app. Any changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any inquiries regarding the privacy policy or how the app handles your data, you can contact the developer via the technical support email provided within the app.
